Let’s support The Home For Single Mothers in Łódź.

Our well-being initiative is collecting products and money to buy necessary items for

The Home For Single Mothers in Łódź.

These women and children are in a difficult financial situation, without money or prospects for life. Often from domestic and psychological violence.

When?

We will collect packages, products and money in the piggy bank located at the reception until May 13, 2024.

What is needed?

Porridges, soups, mousses, juices for children, mineral water, sterile and non-sterile buttons, emollients for bathing and lubrication, teas - chamomile and fennel, intimate hygiene liquids, laundry liquids for white and color, children’s laundry liquids, window cleaners, floor liquids, dishwashing sponges /items, fabric softeners, small and large sweeping brushes, round mops and buckets with a wringer for the round mops.

You can buy products on your own or encourage colleagues from your team

to make a package together.

So join us and let’s be better together!
